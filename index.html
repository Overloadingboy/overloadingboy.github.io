How to Master CyberTic Evolution

This game isn't just about X's and O's; it's about pattern recognition and predictive logic. As you move from Level 1 to Level 100, the AI shifts from random placements to a perfect Minimax algorithm.

Winning Strategies:

  • The Corner Opening: Statistically, starting in a corner gives you the highest number of ways to win.
  • The Fork: Create two ways to win at the same time. This forces the AI to choose one, letting you win with the other.
  • Defense First: At higher levels (Level 50+), the AI will block you instantly. You must play for a draw to survive.
